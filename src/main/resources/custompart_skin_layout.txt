Black areas represent parts reserved for your normal skin.

For Snout/Nub:
- Lime = Front
- Yellow = Top
- Cyan = Bottom (doesn't matter for Nub)
- Purple = Sides

For Tail:
- Lime = Top side
- Yellow = Tail Tips (one is hidden usually but I don't know which one)
- Cyan = Underneath of the tail
- Purple = Sides
